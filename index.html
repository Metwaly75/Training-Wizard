About

Learning design with strategy, not just aesthetics.

I’m Mohamed Metwaly, an instructional designer and corporate learning professional focused on creating practical, engaging, and business-aligned learning experiences.

My work blends structure, creativity, audience awareness, and facilitation thinking to build programs that are not only well-designed, but also easy to deliver and easier to remember.

The “Training Wizard” brand reflects the way I approach learning: making complex topics clearer, workshops more engaging, and training more meaningful for real people.
